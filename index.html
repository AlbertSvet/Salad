Types of salads

1. Vegetable salads

2. Green salads

3. Fruit salads

4. Warm salads

5. Layered salads

6. Сereal salads

  • Vegetable salads

    Salads where the main ingredients are vegetables.

    • -Vinaigrette is a salad made from beets, potatoes, carrots and pickles.
    • -Korean carrots – spicy salad of grated carrots with garlic and spices.

  • Green salads

    The basis of such salads is usually leafy vegetables.

    • -Caesar - with salad, chicken, croutons and Parmesan.
    • -Greek salad – with tomatoes, cucumbers, olives and feta.

  • Fruit saladsa

    The main ingredients are fruits. These salads can be sweet or sweet and sour

    • -Fruit salad – with apples, oranges, bananas and yogurt.
    • -Avocado and strawberry salad – with green leaves and honey mustard dressing.

  • Warm salads

    Salads that use warm ingredients, such as roasted vegetables or meat.

    • -Salad with fried chicken and mushrooms
    • -Salad with warm beets and goat cheese

  • Cereal salads

    Salads based on pasta, cereals or other grains

    • -Italian pasta salad - with pasta, vegetables, olives and cheese.
    • -Tabbouleh salad - with bulgur, parsley, mint and tomatoes.

  • Layered salads

    The ingredients are laid out in layers, often soaked in sauce, like mayonnaise.

    • -Herring under a fur coat - with herring, beets, potatoes and mayonnaise.
    • - Mimosa - salad with canned fish, eggs, carrots and potatoes.
